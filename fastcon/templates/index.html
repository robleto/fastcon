What is Fastcon?

FastCon* is a celebration of the Fast and the Furious franchise with Greg and Selena. Get ready for the opening of F8: The Fate of the Furious by re-watching the first seven films, one per night.

One movie each night for a week

Haven’t seen all the previous Fast and Furious movies? Now’s your chance to catch up with all seven movies leading up to the viewing of part 8 on opening day, April 14th.
